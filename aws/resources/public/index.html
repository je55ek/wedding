Venue

The ceremony and reception will take place outdoors. Please note that there are multiple County Line locations in Austin. Our wedding is at County Line on the Lake, which, confusingly, is NOT on Lake Travis!

Please wear whatever you will be comfortable in outdoors. Temperatures in Austin in mid-April can range from the 60s to the 80s.

In case of rain, the ceremony and reception will move indoors to the large party tent on the County Line property.

Getting Around Town

If you need a ride from the airport or to the wedding, we might be able to match you with a ride from one of our friends! Other local rideshare options like Ride Austin, Fasten and car2go usually offer discounts for your first ride. Of course, the ubiquitous Uber and Lyft serve the Austin area as well.

Also, Capital Metro operates a bus route servicing the Downtown area directly from the Airport.
